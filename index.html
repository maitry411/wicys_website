About Us

At Women in Cybersecurity, we're dedicated to fostering inclusivity and diversity in the rapidly evolving field of cybersecurity.

Our club serves as a supportive community where individuals of all backgrounds can come together to learn, grow, and excel in this dynamic industry.

Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting your journey in cybersecurity, our goal is to provide resources, events, and networking opportunities that empower you to succeed.

Join us as we work towards building a safer digital world, one step at a time.

FAQs

How to join WiCyS AU?

You can Join WiCyS Chapter by clicking on the above Join Chapter button and be a part of community filled with passionate and enthusiastic individuals.

Can only a female student be a part of this?

No, anyone can be a part of this.

Who should join WiCyS AU?

Anyone with a passion for technology, cyber security, and a desire to learn and grow in a supportive community should join WiCyS AU.

What is the purpose of WiCyS?

The mission is to support the WiCyS global mission of recruitment, retention, and advancement of women in cybersecurity. To create a community of professional women in alignment with your mission and passion.

What is the purpose of this Website?

This website serves as a hub for information about the Women in CyberSecurity student chapter at Atmiya University, providing details about our mission, events, and how to join. Along with that you'll see enriching articles by our core team members.
